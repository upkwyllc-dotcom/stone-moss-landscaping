Our Philosophy

We design for the fortieth season, not the first photograph.

Landscaping trends move fast. Land does not. Our work is slower and more deliberate than most — we spend months on drawings before a single stone is set, because a garden rushed at the design stage is a garden that fights you for a decade.

Every project is led end-to-end by a principal designer and built by our own crews, never subcontracted out to the lowest bidder. It's the only way we've found to guarantee the result matches the drawing.

A landscape is the one part of a home that keeps improving after the ribbon is cut — if it's designed to. — Principal, Stone & Moss

How We Work

A deliberate process, kept in-house.

Four stages, one team, zero handoffs. It's slower up front and faster — and far more reliable — for everything after.

01

Consultation

We walk the property together and talk through how you actually want to live outdoors, not just what looks good in a photo.

02

Design

Scaled drawings, planting palettes, and material boards are presented on-site, against the actual light and grade of your land.

03

Craft

In-house crews build to architectural tolerance — the standard we hold ourselves to is our own, not the industry average.

04

Cultivate

Ongoing stewardship keeps the design intact as it matures, season after season, for as long as you'd like us involved.
